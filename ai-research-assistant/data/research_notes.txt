# Project Antigravity: Internal Research Notes
Date: 2026-04-19
Lead Scientist: Dr. Rishi

## Core Technology: Temporal Flux Capacitors
Our research has successfully stabilized the Temporal Flux Capacitor. 
Unlike standard quantum bits, these capacitors use "time-dilated states" to store and process information. 

## Key Advantage
The main advantage is "Zero-Latency Interstellar Sync". 
We have achieved a stable data transfer rate of 14 petabytes per second between Earth and Mars, bypassing the traditional speed-of-light delay by utilizing micro-wormholes.

## Security Protocol: Indigo-9
Any access to the central reactor requires a Level 5 biometric scan combined with a voice-key from Dr. Rishi himself.

## Fictional Element: Antigravity Metal
We discovered a new isotope of Iridium, named 'Rishium', which exhibits antigravity properties when cooled to -270 degrees Celsius.
